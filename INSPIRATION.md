## The Cormorant: A Foraging Expert

### What Are Cormorants?

Cormorants (family Phalacrocoracidae) are freshwater and coastal waterbirds found worldwide. They are expert divers and highly efficient hunters, capable of:

- Diving to depths of 30+ feet
- Pursuing prey underwater at high speed
- Adapting hunting strategies based on prey type
- Collaborating in groups when beneficial
- Managing energy efficiently between hunts

### Why Cormorants?

The PACE Pattern was conceived at **Mill Pond Park in Richmond Hill, Ontario**â€”a local pond where cormorants can be observed fishing. Their behavior sparked the insight:

> Users don't want to browse. They want a guide that dives, finds what they need, and surfaces with the answer.
